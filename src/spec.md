# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Rebuild the AyurGlow Secrets homepage with new content structure, replace the site logo, enhance blog post editing with image upload capabilities, and apply natural wellness design inspiration.

**Planned changes:**
- Update homepage hero section with new headline "Welcome to AyurGlow Secrets" and Ayurvedic wellness subheading
- Add "What We Offer" section featuring four key service areas with emoji icons (health remedies, skin care, hair care, holistic wellness)
- Add "Why AyurGlow Secrets?" section highlighting natural approach, simple remedies, safety, and universal suitability
- Update About page content to reflect the platform's Ayurvedic healing mission and philosophy
- Replace existing site logo with uploaded website logo throughout navigation and footer
- Add image upload capability at the beginning of blog posts in the editor
- Enable image insertion within blog post content via ReactQuill editor toolbar
- Ensure comment section appears at end of blog posts with "Share Your Comment" heading
- Apply visual design inspiration from naturalremedytips.com for layout, typography, and wellness aesthetic while keeping all content unique

**User-visible outcome:** Users will see a redesigned homepage with clear Ayurvedic wellness messaging, new logo branding, and blog post authors can upload and position images throughout their content. Blog readers will have an improved visual experience with a natural wellness design aesthetic.
